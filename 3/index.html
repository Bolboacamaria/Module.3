The term was first recorded in the 10th century, in a Latin manuscript from Gaeta in Central Italy.The modern pizza was invented in Naples, Italy, and the dish and its variants have since become popular in many areas of the world.In 2009, upon Italy's request, Neapolitan pizza was safeguarded in the European Union as a Traditional Speciality Guaranteed dish.The Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana (the True Neapolitan Pizza Association) is a non-profit organization founded in 1984 with headquarters in Naples.
